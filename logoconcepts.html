LOGO DESIGN

Does it look good on print?

I worked on this project in a group with other students: Matthew, Axel, Phuong-Trang, Hana, Maria, and Leah. The objective was to design a logo for a fictitious business/product. We settled on the idea of a melon-flavored soda brand. We called it "Dewdrop."

As I brainstormed with my teammates, I drew most of our conceptual sketches while drawing inspiration from the shapes of honeydew and cantaloupes.

From there, Matthew used Adobe Illustrator to put together a refined vector design based off of the designs we came up with.

Additionally, Maria made some more refined raster designs to conceptualize how the other designs we came up with would look with color.
